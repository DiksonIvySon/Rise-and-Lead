About Us

The Problem We’re Solving

Many young graduates in low-income South African communities struggle to find stable employment or platforms to apply their skills. At the same time, younger learners lack mentorship and guidance from experienced peers. This gap limits both personal growth and community development.

Rise & Lead addresses this by helping graduates build self-sustaining opportunities through entrepreneurship, digital skills, and mentorship — reducing youth unemployment and promoting collaboration across age groups.

“I would like to think that the problem is not that there aren't jobs, it is that there are very few people creating jobs.”
